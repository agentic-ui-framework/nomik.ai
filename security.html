Security & Sovereignty

Powerful agents. You hold the controls.

Control doesn't come from crippling the agents, it comes from making everything visible and putting a human gate on the consequential steps. And you choose where it all runs.

The principle

Open hands, gated consequences.

Inside their own sandbox, agents act freely, that's what makes them useful. The things that touch the outside world or cost real money are proposed by agents and approved by humans.

Capability isn't the risk; unsupervised consequence is. So that's what we gate, and everything is visible while it happens.

The control surface

Everything a human holds.

Approvals (HITL)

Consequential actions are gated, an agent initiates, a human approves. Cards appear inline with full context and Approve / Deny.

Autonomy dial

Per workspace and action-class: suggest-only → one-click → auto-with-notify. Keep money on suggest-only while routine drafting runs automatically.

Spend caps & model ceiling

Ceilings per task / agent / squad / workspace. A model-tier cap (never Opus; Sonnet ceiling; cheap/local for volume) means cost can't silently balloon.

Stop & rate-limit

Stop any turn, session, or the whole fleet, live from chat. Per-thread turn budgets cap runaway loops; pause the workspace at once.

Glass-box audit

Every thought, tool, and dollar is a traced event in #events; a decisions ledger records every approval, durable and reviewable.

Roles & permissions

Owner / Admin / Member, plus scoped agents. The Warden has operator parity minus the human-only acts (approving gated actions, billing identity).

Your workforce, your boundary

Run it where your data is allowed to live.

A capable agent workforce that runs entirely inside your walls is what most agent platforms can't offer. Choose the boundary that fits your risk and compliance.

SaaS

We host it

The fastest start. Nomik-managed, region-pinnable. Cloud models (your keys or managed) or local-via-us.

Private cloud

Your cloud account

Runs inside your VPC and boundary. Your keys or local models in your own infrastructure.

On-prem

Your data center

Never leaves the building. Local / open models on your GPUs, for regulated, public-sector, and air-gapped environments.

Any model, including your own

No prompt, file, or secret has to leave your boundary.

Engines are model-agnostic with a configurable endpoint, so an agent can point at a self-hosted, open-weights model instead of a cloud API. Combined with on-prem, your data never reaches a third-party provider.

  • Local / open models: run the fleet on your own GPUs; bounded, flat compute cost.
  • Best-of-breed where you allow it: frontier cloud model for the hard call, local models for high-volume work.
  • Even voice stays in: self-hosted speech-to-text and text-to-speech keep call audio inside the boundary.
EU & GDPR

Built for the regulated and the sovereign.

Operate in-region with a compliance posture that auditors accept.

  • Data residency: pin processing and storage to the EU or to your own infrastructure.
  • GDPR posture: DPA, lawful basis, data-subject requests, retention controls.
  • Compliance-aware integrations: respect e-invoicing networks, VAT validation, and the like.
  • Isolation & vault: each agent in its own container; secrets scoped to the workspace, granted by you, never held unapproved.
