Legal

Privacy.

How Nomik handles personal data, the lawful bases we rely on, how long we keep it, and how to exercise your rights. Last updated: 23 May 2026.

Who we are

Nomik ("we", "us") is the data controller for personal data processed on this marketing site and for accounts created on the Nomik platform. For questions or to exercise your rights, write to privacy@nomik.ai.

What we collect

  • Marketing site: the email you submit to the waitlist or contact form, and aggregate, cookie-free analytics (page views, country, referrer) from a privacy-friendly analytics tool.
  • Platform accounts: account identifiers (email, name), workspace and team metadata, audit and billing records.
  • Operational data: content you and your agents create inside workspaces (chat, files, decisions, traces). This is processed on your behalf, you control it.
  • Diagnostics: error reports and request logs, kept short-term to operate the service.

Why we process it (lawful bases)

  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): to deliver the service you sign up for, run your workspaces, and bill you.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): to keep the service secure, prevent abuse, and improve features. We balance these against your rights and offer opt-outs where relevant.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): for the waitlist email and any optional marketing communication. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): tax, accounting, and other compliance records.

How long we keep it

  • Waitlist email: until launch or until you ask us to delete it, whichever comes first.
  • Account data: while your account is active, plus a short grace window after closure for backups and legal records (typically 30 to 90 days), then deletion.
  • Workspace content: retained for the lifetime of the workspace; you can delete content or the whole workspace at any time. Backups roll off within 30 days.
  • Billing records: kept for the period required by tax law (typically 7 to 10 years depending on jurisdiction).
  • Logs: short-term, typically 30 days.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access: a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: correct anything that's wrong or out of date.
  • Erasure: ask us to delete your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction and objection: pause or object to specific processing.
  • Withdraw consent: at any time, without affecting prior processing.
  • Complain: to your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these, email privacy@nomik.ai. We'll respond within one month.

Sharing and processors

We use a small set of processors to run the service (hosting, payments, email, support). Each one is bound by a data processing agreement and processes data only on our instructions. On request we can share the current sub-processor list. For Enterprise customers we sign a Data Processing Agreement (see DPA).

International transfers

Where data leaves the EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and equivalent safeguards. On Enterprise plans you can pin region to EU-only or run on-prem so data never leaves your boundary.

Cookies

This marketing site uses no tracking cookies. Analytics is cookie-free and aggregate. See the cookies notice for details.

Changes

If we change this notice we'll update the date at the top and, for material changes, notify account holders by email.
