From a recipe to running

Three steps, mostly the Warden's.

1 · Pick

Choose the work

Search the library or browse a category, read the plain-language brief, and start the one that matches what you keep doing by hand.

2 · Set up

The Warden wires it

It connects anything missing on your grant, confirms how autonomous you want it, asks for the specifics it needs, and names the automation.

3 · Run

It works, and reports back

The squad runs it on schedule or on a trigger, posts a short recap in chat, and brings only the judgment calls to you.
