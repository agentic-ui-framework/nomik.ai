Why AI Agents Can't Pay Today

As AI shifts from reasoning to execution, payments become the bottleneck. Today's rails were built for humans, not software.

No Digital Identity

Banks require human KYC, but agents need programmatic identity verification

→ Without programmatic identity, agents can't access accounts or pass bank onboarding.

Manual Approval Delays

Batch reviews add days of latency. Agents need real-time, pre-approved rules

→ Manual workflows can't keep pace with autonomous systems requiring instant decisions.

Custodial Risk

Traditional fintech requires funds custody, creating liability and control issues

→ Custody brings licensing and liability; teams want control to remain with their bank accounts.

This is why Nomik was built: to let software act financially as fast as it thinks.

Non-Custodial Architecture

03

Policy Engine

Define limits, approvals, sanctions/AML and velocity rules as code. Policies evaluate in real-time with full audit logs and human overrides.

Note: Nomik is not a bank, wallet, or custodian. It routes payments through your existing financial partners.

Developer Quick Start

Drop-in SDKs for your agent framework. Pilot in hours, not months.


import { Nomik } from '@nomik/sdk';
const nomik = new Nomik({ apiKey: process.env.NOMIK_API_KEY });

// Policy-controlled payment
await nomik.executePayment({
  agentId: 'supply_chain_ai',
  amount: 1000,
  currency: 'EUR',
  recipient: 'vendor_001',
  purpose: 'inventory_purchase'
});

The SDK automatically authenticates your agent, applies your policy engine, and routes the transaction through your chosen rail (e.g. SEPA Instant). Responses include policy ID, risk score, and audit log URL.

Or use cURL directly:


curl https://api.nomik.ai/v1/payments \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $NOMIK_API_KEY" \
  -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "agent_id": "supply_chain_ai",
    "amount": 1000,
    "currency": "EUR",
    "recipient": "vendor_001",
    "purpose": "inventory_purchase"
  }'

Verified Regulatory Timeline (EU/US)

  • SEPA Instant (EU): Euro-area PSPs must support instant credit transfers by Oct 9, 2025; further obligations for EMIs/PIs and non-euro members phase in through 2027–2028. ECB Source
  • MiCA (EU): Stablecoin (ART/EMT) regime in force Jun 30, 2024; CASP licensing active Dec 30, 2024; national transition ends Jul 1, 2026. AMF Source
  • EU AI Act: GPAI obligations start Aug 2, 2025; full application by 2027. EU Source
  • FedNow (US): 1,400+ participating institutions as of 2025; real-time payments infrastructure reaching critical mass. Fed Source
  • Data residency: EU and US regions available; customer-controlled selection.
  • PCI scope: Only if card data is processed; by default Nomik does not handle primary account numbers.
  • MiCA & payments: When stablecoin rails are used for payments, settlement occurs via licensed partners; separate payment/EMI licensing may be required by the operator depending on the model.
  • AI Act readiness: Nomik operates as a General-Purpose AI system component provider (GPAI) under Article 52 (2024/1689), offering transparency and human oversight controls.
  • Audit exports: All policy decisions exportable in JSON/CSV for regulatory filing (PSD2 RTS on SCA & CSC).

Regulatory Note: Requirements vary by jurisdiction; Nomik operates as a non-custodial software provider. Settlement occurs through your existing bank or EMI partner.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nomik a bank or EMI?

No. Nomik is a non-custodial policy and routing layer. Funds remain with your bank/EMI partners.

What is KYA?

Know-Your-Agent: verifying and binding agents to a principal with revocable scopes.

How fast are policy checks?

Sub-100ms under load (internal benchmarks); details in documentation.

Which payment rails are supported?

SEPA Instant, FedNow, SWIFT; optional stablecoins where regulation permits.

How do you handle AML/sanctions?

Policy-as-code integrates with accredited providers; all decisions are auditable.

What does non-custodial mean?

Funds stay with your bank/EMI; Nomik does not custody. We route and enforce policy only.

Which licenses cover settlement?

Settlement occurs through your existing bank or EMI partner; Nomik does not hold client funds.

Do I need my own banking partner to use Nomik?

Yes. Nomik connects your agents to payments through your existing bank or any EMI within our partner network. If you don't have one, we'll match you with a licensed provider.

Can Nomik be white-labeled?

Yes. Enterprise clients can integrate Nomik's policy engine and network access under their own brand with full API control.

Do you store private keys?

No; we do not store client private keys. Keys remain with you or your HSM provider.

Do you act as custodian?

No; funds remain with your bank/EMI partner.

Do you support MiCA stablecoins?

Yes, via pilot programs with MiCA-aligned stablecoins (e.g., EURC) through licensed partners for on/off-ramp settlement.

What is your AI Act obligation scope?

Nomik operates as a General-Purpose AI component provider under Article 52, providing transparency tools and human oversight controls for compliance.
