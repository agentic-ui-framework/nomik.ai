Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nomik?

Nomik is an agentic neobank that unifies CFO workflows and developer automation. Finance teams define policies, approvals, and treasury logic, while developers build autonomous agents that execute under those same controls.

Who defines workflows in Nomik?

CFOs and controllers define policies through a visual interface. Developers extend those policies through SDKs and APIs. This ensures all automation remains under financial governance.

How is Nomik different from automation tools like Make.com or Zapier?

Automation tools can connect apps, but they lack financial identity, policy enforcement, and auditability. Nomik embeds automation inside the banking layer where every workflow runs under policy, compliance, and real-time monitoring. It's the difference between an automation script and a regulated programmable bank.

Who uses Nomik?

CFOs and Controllers use Nomik to automate payments, approvals, and treasury operations safely. Developers and Finance Engineers build agents and integrations that act under CFO-defined policies. Nomik bridges both roles through one unified, compliant execution environment.

How does the CFO experience work?

CFOs create and test workflows in a no-code policy builder. They define thresholds, approvals, and treasury logic in natural language, then simulate and enforce them directly within accounts and rails. Every workflow is versioned, auditable, and compliant by default.

How does the developer experience work?

Developers extend CFO-defined workflows using Nomik's APIs and SDKs. They can build autonomous agents, connect ERPs, or trigger policy-guarded transactions from AI systems, all inheriting the same governance and audit logic.

Is Nomik a licensed bank?

Nomik operates as a regulated neobank under electronic-money and banking-as-a-service frameworks in partnership with licensed financial institutions. It supports full KYC, AML, and safeguarding compliance while maintaining the flexibility of an agentic infrastructure bank.

What kinds of accounts and payments are supported?

Nomik provides multi-currency operating and treasury accounts with support for SEPA Instant, SWIFT, FedNow, and ACH; MiCA-aligned stablecoins and tokenized deposits; and instant internal transfers between Nomik accounts and agents.

What is Policy-as-Code?

It's how Nomik turns finance rules into executable logic. Example: "Payments above €10,000 require CFO approval." Nomik enforces this automatically across every system, currency, and workflow.

What is KYA (Know Your Agent)?

KYA extends KYC/KYB to autonomous systems. It identifies each agent, defines its permissions, and tracks every action through signed audit events. That means CFOs and auditors always know who executed a transaction, even when it's a bot.

How secure is Nomik's infrastructure?

Nomik runs on ISO 27001 and SOC 2-aligned environments, encrypts all data in transit and at rest, and logs every transaction in a cryptographically signed ledger. It also integrates directly with enterprise SIEM tools such as Datadog and Splunk for continuous monitoring.

Can Nomik be deployed privately?

Yes. Enterprise clients can run Nomik in dedicated cloud or on-premises deployments to ensure data sovereignty and full internal control.

What regions does Nomik serve?

Nomik serves clients across Europe, the United Kingdom, the Middle East, and Asia, with expansion planned to North America. All operations align with FATF, MiCA, and ISO international standards.

How can my team get access?

Nomik is onboarding select finance teams and integration partners. Contact hello@nomik.ai or apply to the early-access program.

Summary

Nomik bridges automation and trust. CFOs define how money moves. Developers define how intelligence acts. Together they run on one agentic banking core: compliant, auditable, and under full control.
