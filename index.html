LangGraph Integration Example

from langgraph import StateGraph
from nomik_langgraph import PaymentNode, PolicyGuardNode

# Define payment workflow
workflow = StateGraph(AgentState)
workflow.add_node("policy_check", PolicyGuardNode(nomik_client))
workflow.add_node("execute_payment", PaymentNode(nomik_client))
workflow.add_edge("policy_check", "execute_payment")

# Handle approvals
workflow.add_conditional_edges(
    "policy_check",
    lambda state: "require_approval" if state.needs_approval else "approved"
)

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about agent payments and Nomik.ai.

Is Nomik.ai a bank?

No, Nomik.ai is a non-custodial policy & routing layer. We don't hold funds; they stay at your bank or PSP.

Which payment rails are supported?

SEPA (Instant/SCT), FedNow/ACH, SWIFT, and select stablecoins. Availability varies by region and partner participation.

How do you prevent agent abuse?

KYA scopes, spending limits, recipient allowlists, approval workflows, and real-time anomaly alerts.

Can we host Nomik.ai privately?

Yes, Enterprise plans include VPC and on-premises deployment options.

What is KYA vs KYC?

KYA (Know Your Agent) establishes agent identity & delegation rights, complementing traditional KYC/KYB for the underlying business.

What happens if a policy blocks a critical payment?

Human override capabilities with full audit trail. Critical payments can be approved manually while maintaining compliance.
