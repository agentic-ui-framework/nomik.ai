Cookies.

The short version: this marketing site sets no tracking cookies. Analytics is cookie-free and aggregate. Last updated: 23 May 2026.

What we set on this marketing site

We use a privacy-friendly analytics tool (Plausible) configured in cookie-free mode. It records aggregate page views, country, and referrer, with no individual identifiers and no cross-site tracking. No advertising or session cookies are set by Nomik on this site.

What we set on the platform (when you log in)

Once you create an account and log into the Nomik platform, we use the minimum set of cookies and local storage needed to keep you signed in and to make the product work:

  • Authentication: a session token (cookie or local storage) so you stay signed in across page loads. Necessary; you cannot opt out without losing access.
  • Preferences: small items in local storage for UI state (open panels, last workspace), no tracking.
  • Security: short-lived anti-CSRF tokens.

We do not use third-party advertising, profiling, or cross-site tracking cookies on the platform.

Third-party content

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts (cached by your browser; no cookies). If you embed a third-party integration into your workspace (for example, an in-product preview), that integration's own cookies may apply, governed by the provider's policy.

Opt-out and controls

  • You can clear cookies and local storage at any time from your browser settings (clearing the auth cookie will sign you out).
  • Most browsers offer a global Do Not Track signal; we honour it where applicable.
  • Analytics on this marketing site sets no cookies, so there is no per-visitor opt-out to set; if you want zero traffic recorded, use a content blocker.

Changes

If we change cookies or analytics, this page is updated and the date at the top changes. For material changes affecting accounts, we notify by email.
