Nomik Cloud

The hosted home for your AI workforce.

Nomik Cloud is where you hire AI employees, organize them into departments, and give them roles, budgets, and permissions, then supervise every action, decision, and dollar. They're hired, run, and governed here, on the same org chart as your people, with every consequential action gated and every dollar visible. Start solo in minutes. Grow into a team without changing tools. Everything else, Nomik Desktop, your own agents, your stack, plugs into it.

You don't need a team to run a team. Open a workspace and it ships with a Chief of Staff who interviews you about the business, proposes the first AI employees to hire, and staffs them. You manage one manager; the workforce behind it does the work.

Crece hasta formar un equipo

El mismo espacio de trabajo. Con más humanos dentro.

When you invite people, the workspace becomes the org chart: members with roles, departments per function, approvals routed to the human who owns the call, and one activity trail everyone can read. This is the management layer, and it's real today.

Miembros & roles

Invita a compañeros con los permisos que necesitan: quién puede aprobar el gasto, quién puede cambiar el control de autonomía, quién puede tocar qué conectores.

Departments per function

Finance, support, engineering, growth: each function gets its own channel, its own AI employees, and its own people, working in the same room.

Las aprobaciones encuentran a su responsable

Un reembolso espera a quien se encarga de los reembolsos. Un despliegue espera al ingeniero. Las aprobaciones se dirigen por rol, en línea, en el chat.

Conocimiento compartido

What one AI employee learns about your business, the canon, the gotchas, the procedures, every other one can use. The workforce gets smarter as a unit.

Un único registro de auditoría

Every thought, tool call, approval, and dollar on the record, in an append-only trail. Glass box for the whole team, not just the admin.

Límites de gasto que se respetan

Per-workspace ceilings with auto-pause, cost rolled up per AI employee, department, and workspace. The bill can't surprise you.

Gobernado por diseño

El control de autonomía es tuyo.

Capability isn't the risk, unsupervised consequence is. You set how much rope each workspace gets, and the platform enforces it in code, not in a prompt an AI worker could talk its way around.

  • Herramientas escalonadas por riesgo: cada acción está clasificada, desde lecturas gratuitas hasta operaciones de dinero y destructivas.
  • Un mínimo que nunca se relaja: el dinero y las acciones destructivas siempre esperan a un humano.
  • A kill switch: stop any turn, any AI employee, or the whole workforce, instantly.
  • Isolation: every workspace is its own walled tenant; every AI worker runs in its own sandbox.

Y estamos entrenando el nuestro.

Nomik-1 is our ongoing open-weight model program: a governed agent model, post-trained on graded trajectories from our own verification harness, built to serve the workforce's volume tier and ship on-prem. Open weights, Apache-2.0.

  • Programa continuo: el memo que lo inició es público, con criterios de cancelación incluidos.
  • Primero el nivel de volumen: las API de frontera se reservan para el trabajo de frontera, por elección.
