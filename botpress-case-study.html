The Conversational Commerce Revolution

Botpress powers millions of chatbots worldwide with their open-source conversational AI platform. They've mastered natural language understanding, but there's a critical gap: chatbots can't complete commerce transactions.

Every day, millions of chatbot conversations end at "Would you like to purchase?" because they lack payment capabilities.

The Commerce Completion Gap

  • • Conversation Abandonment: 73% of purchase intent conversations never complete
  • • Manual Handoff Required: Humans must step in for all financial transactions
  • • Complex Payment Integration: Each commerce use case requires custom development
  • • No Subscription Management: Recurring payments handled outside chatbot experience
  • • Limited Global Reach: International payment processing barriers

Technical Solution: Conversational Payment Agents

1. Native Commerce Integration

Botpress chatbots gain built-in payment capabilities through Nomik's conversational commerce SDK.

// Register commerce-enabled chatbot agent
const commerceBot = await nomik.kya.registerConversationalAgent({
identity: `did:ai:botpress:commerce:${botId}`,
platform: "botpress",
capabilities: [
"conversational_payment_processing",
"cart_management",
"subscription_handling",
"refund_processing"
]
});
