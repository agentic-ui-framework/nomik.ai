15,000+ suppliers €500M monthly procurement 23 countries

The Challenge

AutoSupply's procurement team was drowning in manual approval processes. With 15,000+ suppliers across 23 countries, their finance team spent 8 hours daily approving routine payments, creating bottlenecks that delayed supplier payments by an average of 14 days. This manual process cost €2.8M annually in operational overhead and strained supplier relationships.

Manual Bottlenecks

200+ daily payment approvals requiring human review

High Operational Costs

€2.8M annual overhead for manual payment processing

Supplier Relations

14-day payment delays damaging supplier partnerships

The Nomik Solution

AutoSupply implemented Nomik's agent-native payment infrastructure, enabling their procurement AI agents to autonomously process supplier payments while maintaining full compliance and control. The solution included automated policy enforcement, real-time fraud detection, and seamless integration with their existing SAP system.

Customer Context

Fortune 500 automotive manufacturer with 15,000+ suppliers across 23 countries processing €500M monthly procurement volume. Complex supply chain with seasonal variance and regulatory requirements across multiple jurisdictions.

Business Constraints

Risk limits: €50K single transaction limit, €200K daily velocity cap per supplier. Manual approvals required for new suppliers and cross-border payments >€25K. Peak volume: 1,200 transactions/day during production cycles.

Technical Integration

Systems: SAP S/4HANA integration via Nomik JavaScript SDK. Pilot duration: 4 weeks. Team: 2 developers, 1 compliance officer, 1 treasury manager. Banking partner: Deutsche Bank (SEPA Instant enabled).

Policy Configuration

Sample rules: Per-invoice limit €50K, blocked countries (sanctioned territories), velocity cap €200K/day/supplier, auto-approval for trusted suppliers with valid PO matching, escalation for anomalies >2σ from baseline.

Quantified Results

Policy decisions: <100ms p50 latency (internal benchmarks); Operational time saved: 1,500 hours/month; Approval success rate: 99.2%; Financial impact: 2.3% early payment discount capture, €180K monthly working capital optimization.
