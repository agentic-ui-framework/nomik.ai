LangGraph Integration Example

from langgraph import StateGraph
from nomik_langgraph import PaymentNode, PolicyGuardNode

# Define payment workflow
workflow = StateGraph(AgentState)
workflow.add_node("policy_check", PolicyGuardNode(nomik_client))
workflow.add_node("execute_payment", PaymentNode(nomik_client))
workflow.add_edge("policy_check", "execute_payment")

# Handle approvals
workflow.add_conditional_edges(
    "policy_check",
    lambda state: "require_approval" if state.needs_approval else "approved"
)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nomik?

Nomik is a programmable neobank designed for institutional and autonomous finance. It provides multi-rail accounts, treasury automation, and policy-driven execution, allowing finance teams and autonomous systems to move funds securely under defined rules.

What does "programmable" mean in banking?

It means your financial logic becomes executable. Nomik lets you define approval workflows, payment limits, or FX strategies in policy form, then enforces them automatically across every transaction, account, and rail.

What types of accounts does Nomik offer?

Nomik provides multi-currency operating and treasury accounts with full API access. Each account can support SEPA, SWIFT, and stablecoin rails under the same policy layer. Segregated sub-accounts and delegated access are available for subsidiaries, teams, or automated agents.

Is Nomik licensed as a bank?

Nomik operates under neobank and electronic-money licensing frameworks in partnership with regulated financial institutions. It provides full KYC, AML, and safeguarding compliance while maintaining the agility of a technology-first infrastructure bank.

How does Nomik ensure compliance?

Compliance is built into every operation. Nomik automates onboarding (KYC/KYB), transaction monitoring, anomaly detection, and FATF-aligned reporting. All policies, transactions, and approvals are logged in encrypted, immutable audit trails.

How are funds protected?

Client funds are held in segregated safeguarded accounts with top-tier banking partners. Nomik maintains full reconciliation and daily verification under e-money and payment-institution standards.

What payment rails are supported?

Nomik connects traditional and digital rails under one architecture: Bank Rails (SEPA, SWIFT, FedNow, ACH), Digital Rails (USDC/EURC, tokenized deposits, MiCA-aligned stablecoins), and Internal Routing (instant transfers between Nomik accounts and agents).

What is Policy-as-Code?

Policy-as-Code turns your finance rules into enforceable logic. Example: "Payments above €10,000 require CFO approval." Nomik executes that rule automatically with no manual intervention, no missed control.

What is KYA (Know Your Agent)?

KYA extends traditional KYC/KYB to autonomous systems. It defines identity, scope, and behavioral parameters for agents or algorithms acting within your organization. Each agent's actions are monitored, audited, and controlled under the same policy layer.

Can Nomik operate fully autonomously?

Yes, within your governance rules. Nomik can automate payments, treasury rebalancing, FX, and reporting. Every automated action remains traceable, reversible, and compliant with human-defined policies.

How secure is Nomik's infrastructure?

Nomik is built on ISO 27001 and SOC 2-aligned environments, with full encryption in transit and at rest. All policy and transaction data are isolated per organization. Each action generates a signed ledger event for audit integrity.

Can Nomik be deployed privately?

Yes. Nomik offers dedicated cloud or on-premises deployments for enterprise and regulated institutions requiring data sovereignty and internal control over financial automation.

Who is Nomik designed for?

Nomik serves CFOs, controllers, and financial developers managing multi-entity or high-volume operations. It's for organizations seeking bank-grade infrastructure that adapts to autonomous, policy-driven execution.

What regions does Nomik serve?

Nomik supports clients across Europe, the United Kingdom, the Middle East, and Asia, with planned expansion to North America. All operations comply with cross-border regulatory frameworks and international data standards.

How can I get access?

Nomik is currently onboarding select finance teams and partners. To request access, contact hello@nomik.ai or apply via the early-access program.
