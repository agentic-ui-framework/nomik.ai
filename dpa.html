Data Processing Agreement.

This page is a public summary, marked DRAFT. For a signed DPA suitable for procurement, email legal@nomik.ai. Last updated: 23 May 2026.

Roles

For workspace content you put into Nomik, you (the customer) are the data controller and Nomik is the data processor. We process personal data on your documented instructions, which are reflected in the service configuration (workspaces, members, integrations, deployment tier).

Scope and subject matter

Nomik processes personal data only to provide the platform: hosting workspaces, running agents, executing approved actions, logging the glass-box audit trail, sending notifications, and billing.

Categories of data subjects and data

  • Subjects: your employees, contractors, customers, prospects, and other people whose data flows through your workspaces.
  • Data: identifiers, contact details, content you and your agents create, communications, files you upload, and the operational metadata required to run the service.
  • Special categories: Nomik is not designed for special-category data; if your use requires it, talk to us so we can scope the deployment (private cloud or on-prem).

Sub-processors

We use a limited set of sub-processors (hosting, payments, email, support, observability). Each is bound by a back-to-back DPA. The current list is available on request and notice of changes is given to customers in advance, with a right to object.

Security measures

  • Tenant isolation: per-workspace boundaries, scoped credentials, vault for secrets.
  • Encryption: in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Access control: least privilege, audit logs, MFA for staff.
  • Glass-box audit: every consequential action by a human or agent is recorded.
  • Backups: encrypted, time-bound retention, regular restore tests.
  • Incident response: defined playbook with notification to affected customers without undue delay (and within 72 hours where required).

International transfers

Where data leaves the EEA we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and equivalent safeguards. Enterprise customers can pin region to the EU or deploy on-prem so data never leaves their boundary.

Data-subject requests

We assist controllers in responding to data-subject requests (access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, objection) through documented endpoints and, where needed, manual operator support.

Retention and deletion

We retain personal data for as long as the workspace exists. On termination of the contract or on a documented deletion request, we delete the relevant data within 30 days, subject to legal retention obligations. Backups roll off within 30 days.

Audits

Enterprise customers can request audit information (security questionnaires, certifications, sub-processor list). On-site audits are scoped on a case-by-case basis with reasonable notice.

Liability

Liability under the DPA follows the limits in the main agreement (see terms), subject to the carve-outs required by GDPR.

Request a signed copy

Email legal@nomik.ai with your company name and intended use. We'll send the current DPA template, including the SCCs and the sub-processor list.
